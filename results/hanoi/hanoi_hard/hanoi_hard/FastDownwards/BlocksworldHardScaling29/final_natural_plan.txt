This plan details the steps required to solve the 6-disk Tower of Hanoi puzzle, moving the entire stack of disks (A, B, C, D, E, F) from the Right Peg (R) to the Left Peg (L). Disk A is the smallest, and Disk F is the largest.

## Action Sequence

1.  Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Middle Peg (M).
2.  Move Disk B (on Disk C) to the Left Peg (L).
3.  Move Disk A (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk B.
4.  Move Disk C (on Disk D) to the Middle Peg (M).
5.  Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk D.
6.  Move Disk B (from Left Peg L) onto Disk C.
7.  Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
8.  Move Disk D (on Disk E) to the Left Peg (L).
9.  Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk E.
10. Move Disk A (on Disk E) onto Disk D.
11. Move Disk B (on Disk C) onto Disk E.
12. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
13. Move Disk C (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk D.
14. Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Middle Peg (M).
15. Move Disk B (on Disk E) onto Disk C.
16. Move Disk A (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk B.
17. Move Disk E (on Disk F) to the Middle Peg (M).
18. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk F.
19. Move Disk B (on Disk C) onto Disk E.
20. Move Disk A (on Disk F) onto Disk B.
21. Move Disk C (on Disk D) onto Disk F.
22. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk D.
23. Move Disk B (on Disk E) onto Disk C.
24. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
25. Move Disk D (from Left Peg L) onto Disk E.
26. Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Left Peg (L).
27. Move Disk A (from Left Peg L) onto Disk D.
28. Move Disk B (on Disk C) to the Left Peg (L).
29. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
30. Move Disk C (on Disk F) onto Disk D.
31. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk F.
32. Move Disk B (from Left Peg L) onto Disk C.
33. Move Disk A (on Disk F) onto Disk B.
34. Move Disk F (from Right Peg R) to the Left Peg (L).
35. Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Right Peg (R).
36. Move Disk A (from Right Peg R) onto Disk F.
37. Move Disk B (on Disk C) to the Right Peg (R).
38. Move Disk A (on Disk F) onto Disk B.
39. Move Disk C (on Disk D) onto Disk F.
40. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk D.
41. Move Disk B (from Right Peg R) onto Disk C.
42. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
43. Move Disk D (on Disk E) to the Right Peg (R).
44. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk E.
45. Move Disk A (on Disk E) onto Disk D.
46. Move Disk B (on Disk C) onto Disk E.
47. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
48. Move Disk C (on Disk F) onto Disk D.
49. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk F.
50. Move Disk B (on Disk E) onto Disk C.
51. Move Disk A (on Disk F) onto Disk B.
52. Move Disk E (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk F.
53. Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Middle Peg (M).
54. Move Disk B (on Disk C) onto Disk E.
55. Move Disk A (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk B.
56. Move Disk C (on Disk D) to the Middle Peg (M).
57. Move Disk A (on Disk B) onto Disk D.
58. Move Disk B (on Disk E) onto Disk C.
59. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
60. Move Disk D (from Right Peg R) onto Disk E.
61. Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Right Peg (R).
62. Move Disk A (from Right Peg R) onto Disk D.
63. Move Disk B (on Disk C) to the Right Peg (R).
64. Move Disk A (on Disk D) onto Disk B.
65. Move Disk C (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk D.
66. Move Disk A (on Disk B) to the Middle Peg (M).
67. Move Disk B (from Right Peg R) onto Disk C.
68. Move Disk A (from Middle Peg M) onto Disk B.

The goal state (Stack F/E/D/C/B/A on the Left Peg L) is now achieved.
