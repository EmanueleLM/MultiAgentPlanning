This is a plan to solve the Tower of Hanoi puzzle involving 5 disks (A, B, C, D, E) and 3 pegs (L, M, R), moving the stack from Peg M to Peg R. Disk A is the smallest and E is the largest.

### **Plan for Tower of Hanoi (5 Disks: M -> R)**

1.  Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg L.
2.  Move disk B from on top of disk C to peg R.
3.  Move disk A from peg L onto disk B.
4.  Move disk C from on top of disk D to peg L.
5.  Move disk A from on top of disk B onto disk D.
6.  Move disk B from peg R onto disk C.
7.  Move disk A from on top of disk D onto disk B.
8.  Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg R.
9.  Move disk B from on top of disk C onto disk D.
10. Move disk A from peg R onto disk B.
11. Move disk C from peg L to peg R.
12. Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg L.
13. Move disk B from on top of disk D onto disk C.
14. Move disk A from peg L onto disk B.
15. Move disk D from on top of disk E to peg L.
16. Move disk A from on top of disk B onto disk E.
17. Move disk A from on top of disk E onto disk D.
18. Move disk B from on top of disk C onto disk E.
19. Move disk A from on top of disk D onto disk B.
20. Move disk C from peg R onto disk D.
21. Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg R.
22. Move disk B from on top of disk E onto disk C.
23. Move disk A from peg R onto disk B.
24. Move disk E from peg M to peg R.
25. Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg M.
26. Move disk B from on top of disk C onto disk E.
27. Move disk A from peg M onto disk B.
28. Move disk C from on top of disk D to peg M.
29. Move disk A from on top of disk B onto disk D.
30. Move disk B from on top of disk E onto disk C.
31. Move disk A from on top of disk D onto disk B.
32. Move disk D from peg L onto disk E.
33. Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg L.
34. Move disk A from peg L onto disk D.
35. Move disk B from on top of disk C to peg L.
36. Move disk A from on top of disk D onto disk B.
37. Move disk C from peg M onto disk D.
38. Move disk A from on top of disk B to peg M.
39. Move disk B from peg L onto disk C.
40. Move disk A from peg M onto disk B.
