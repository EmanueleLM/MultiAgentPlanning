This task involves solving the 4-disk Tower of Hanoi puzzle, moving all disks (A, B, C, D, where A is the smallest and D is the largest) from the initial Right peg to the final Left peg, using the Middle peg as auxiliary.

Here is the sequence of 16 actions required to complete the puzzle:

1.  **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Right peg to the empty Middle peg.
2.  **Move Disk B:** Move Disk B from the Right peg to the empty Left peg.
3.  **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Middle peg onto Disk B on the Left peg.
4.  **Move Disk C:** Move Disk C from the Right peg to the empty Middle peg.
5.  **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Left peg onto Disk D on the Right peg.
6.  **Move Disk B:** Move Disk B from the Left peg onto Disk C on the Middle peg.
7.  **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Right peg onto Disk B on the Middle peg.
8.  **Move Disk D:** Move Disk D from the Right peg to the empty Left peg.
9.  **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Middle peg onto Disk D on the Left peg.
10. **Move Disk B:** Move Disk B from the Middle peg to the empty Right peg.
11. **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Left peg onto Disk C on the Middle peg.
12. **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Middle peg onto Disk B on the Right peg.
13. **Move Disk C:** Move Disk C from the Middle peg onto Disk D on the Left peg.
14. **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Right peg to the empty Middle peg.
15. **Move Disk B:** Move Disk B from the Right peg onto Disk C on the Left peg.
16. **Move Disk A:** Move Disk A from the Middle peg onto Disk B on the Left peg.

The goal is achieved. All four disks are now stacked correctly (A/B/C/D, top to bottom) on the Left peg.
